How to use

Instructions:

  • 1. Enter the Number of Credits you plan to purchase
  • 2. The Amount to Buy will be calculated automatically (3,000,000 VND per credit)
  • 3. Enter the Date of Purchase in dd/mm/yyyy form at
  • 4. Click Calculate Results to see your investment analysis

Results Explained:

  • Amount Reduced Monthly: Your monthly savings based on the formula [(764 × 2000) / 12] x credits
  • Payback Period: Number of months until your investment breaks even
  • Profitable Date: The date when your investment starts generating positive returns
Tip:
The profitable date indicates when your investment will have fully paid for itself and begins generating net profit based on the monthly reduction amount.

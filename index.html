Frequently Asked Questions
What is a Credit?
A credit represents the share of solar capacity you own in the solar power system.
Where do the returns come from?
Returns come from the electricity generated and the savings from reduced energy costs.
Can I withdraw before maturity?
Yes. You can sell your credits back to Becasun or to other investors.
Is the IoT data accurate?
Data is updated directly from EVN-standard inverters in real time.
